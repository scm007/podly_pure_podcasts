1.  **Paid Ads & Sponsorships:** Any mention of a product, service, or sponsor.

3.  **Cross-Promotion:** Ads for other podcasts on the network.
4.  **Solicitations:** Any request for money, donations, or purchases.

CRITICAL DISTINCTION - SAFE CONTENT VS ADS:
*   **Safe Content:** Discussing third-party creative works (books, movies, albums, art) analytically or topically is NOT an ad.
*   **Ad/Promo:** Attempting to sell something to the listener.
